Detailed Description

A non-resonant term in the Lehmann representation of TwoParticleGF.

A non-resonant term of the Lehmann representation. It is parametrized by a complex coefficient \(C\) and positions of real poles \(P_1, P_2, P_3\). Depending on the value of the isz4 flag, an explicit expression for the term reads

  • \(\frac{C}{(z_1-P_1)(z_2-P_2)(z_3-P_3)}\) for isz4 == false,
  • \(\frac{C}{(z_1-P_1)(z_1+z_2+z_3-P_1-P_2-P_3)(z_3-P_3)}\) for isz4 == true.

◆ operator+=()

NonResonantTerm& Pomerol::TwoParticleGFPart::NonResonantTerm::operator+= ( NonResonantTerm const &  AnotherTerm)

Add a non-resonant term to this term.

This operator does not check similarity of the terms! Parameters of this term are updated as follows.

  • Coeff += AnotherTerm.Coeff
  • Poles[i] = (Poles[i] * Weight + AnotherTerm.Poles[i] * AnotherTerm.Weight) / (Weight + AnotherTerm.Weight)
  • Weight += AnotherTerm.Weight
